> placeholders with your own brand. The file `docs/brand.md` is intentionally

> Claude Code or Codex enough direction to keep your site consistent. You can

## Color Usage

Recommended defaults:

- Background: `[#ffffff]`
- Primary text: `[#222222]`
- Headings: `[#000000]`
- Primary button: `[#000000]` with `[#ffffff]` text
- Secondary button: transparent with `[#000000]` border
- Accent details: `[#000000]`

## Layout Principles

The layout should feel [spacious, calm, and structured].

Use:

- Clear page sections
- Generous whitespace
- Strong alignment
- Reusable components
- Clear calls to action

Avoid:

- Decorative clutter
- Overlapping text
- Generic template sameness
- Complex animations for basic information

## Page-Level Brand Guidance

### Home

Make the promise obvious near the top:

- [What you do]
- [Who it's for]
- [The next step you want visitors to take]

### About

Establish trust through clarity, not inflated credentials:

- Who this is for
- Why your approach matters
- A grounded personal story or philosophy

### Work With Me / Services

Keep this page clear and action-oriented:

- Who the offer is for
- What problem it solves
- What is included
- How the process works
- What the next step is

### Blog

Describe the kind of posts you'll write and the topics you'll cover.

### Contact

Keep contact simple:

- A clear invitation
- Contact form or email link
- Booking link, if relevant
- Expectations for response time

## Component Guidance

Buttons:

- Primary buttons should use [your primary color].
- Secondary buttons should be outlined.
- Use your accent color sparingly for high-attention moments.

Navigation:

- Keep nav labels plain.
- Recommended nav: Home, About, Work With Me, Blog, Contact.

Forms:

- Inputs should be simple, legible, and calm.
- Use clear labels, not placeholder-only labels.

## Accessibility

- Maintain strong color contrast.
- Do not use light accent colors as body text on light backgrounds.
- Make buttons and links visually distinct.
- Keep body text comfortably sized.
- Make focus states visible.

## AI Agent Instructions

When an AI coding agent changes the site:

- Use this brand guide as the source of truth for visual and writing direction.
- Keep the palette, voice, and layout principles consistent.
- Make small, targeted updates.
- Preserve readability, crawlability, and maintainability.
